Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 5, 2026

Spendova ("the App") is developed by Seifeddine Bouzid. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Data Collection

Spendova does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. The App operates 100% offline. All financial data (transactions, budgets, savings goals, settings) is stored locally on your device using on-device storage.

2. No Accounts or Sign-Up

The App does not require account creation, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personal identifiers. There is no login system.

3. No Analytics or Tracking

Spendova does not use any analytics frameworks, crash reporters, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies. No usage data leaves your device.

4. No Network Requests

The App makes no network requests except for:

  • In-App Purchases: Subscription verification is handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework via RevenueCat. Only purchase receipt validation occurs, and this is managed by Apple — no financial data is shared with the developer.

5. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data is sandboxed to the App and is not accessible by other apps. When you delete the App, all data is permanently removed.

6. Backup & Export

You may choose to export your data as an encrypted file (.spendova). This file is encrypted using AES-256 encryption. The export is initiated solely by you and shared via your device's native share sheet. The developer has no access to exported files.

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses RevenueCat solely for managing in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy. No personal data beyond Apple's anonymous transaction identifiers is processed.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children, as the App collects no data at all.

9. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
